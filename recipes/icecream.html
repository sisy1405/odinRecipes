Vanilla Icecream

This is a simple vanilla ice cream recipe, but very tasty, it's definitely a must

Ingredients

  • 4 cups half-and-half or light cream.
  • 1 can condensed milk (14 ounce).
    • 2 tablespoons vanilla extract.

steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine all ingredients; mix well
  2. Pour into ice cream freezer container and freeze according to manufacturer's instructions.
