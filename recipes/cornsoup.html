Corn Soup

This soup is really easy to make, lower in fat than most cream soups, and deliciuos, especially if you are fighting off a cold!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 onion, chopped.
  • 1 clove garlic, minced.
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley.
  • 1 tablespoon margarine.
  • 3 tablespoon all-purpose flour.
  • 2 1/2 cups milk.
  • 1 cup chicken broth.
  • 2 cans whole kernel corn, drained (12 ounce).
  • 2 1/2 tablespoon cream cheese.
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt.
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper.
  • Ground cayenne pepper to taste.

Steps

  1. In a large pot over medium heat, combine the onion, garlic, parslet and butter or margarine. Saute for about 5 minutes, or until onions are tender.
  2. Add the fluor, stirring well, to make a pasty mixture. Whisk in the milk and the broth.
  3. Add the corn and the cream cheese and allow to heat through. Add the garlic salt, black pepper and cayenne pepper to taste. Stir together and serve.
